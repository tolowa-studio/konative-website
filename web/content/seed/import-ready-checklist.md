# Import-Ready Checklist

Use this checklist when importing or syncing Konative content into Sanity, Builder.io, or another CMS surface.

## Pre-Import
- [ ] Confirm source content reflects connectivity intelligence and brokerage positioning.
- [ ] Confirm no current content describes Konative as an outdoor-living, surfaces, or manufacturer-representation business.
- [ ] Confirm primary CTA links point to `/contact`.
- [ ] Confirm warm-intent booking links use `https://cal.com/jeramey-james`.
- [ ] Confirm colors use the current black/steel/velocity-red system.

## Post-Import Validation
- [ ] Open `/`, `/connectivity`, `/datacenters`, `/intelligence`, `/markets`, `/news`, and `/contact`.
- [ ] Confirm page titles, metadata, and CTAs match current positioning.
- [ ] Confirm mobile layouts do not overflow.
- [ ] Confirm forms submit or fail visibly.
