# Services Page Content

## 1) Connectivity Brokerage
Konative sources internet, fiber, dark fiber, wavelengths, transport, SD-WAN, voice, cloud connectivity, and managed network services across a broad supplier portfolio. Buyers get market comparison, commercial guidance, and one accountable advisor.

**What's included**
- Requirements intake and supplier matching
- Carrier-neutral quote comparison
- Contract and term review support
- Order, provisioning, and escalation coordination

**Target audience**
- Enterprise buyers, datacenter operators, tribal enterprises, multi-site organizations

## 2) Datacenter Connectivity
Konative supports datacenter developers, operators, and AI infrastructure teams with early route analysis, supplier discovery, interconnection options, dark fiber sourcing, and cloud on-ramp planning.

**What's included**
- Fiber route and carrier availability review
- Dark fiber, wavelength, DIA, and interconnection sourcing
- Site-stage connectivity analysis
- Supplier shortlist and procurement support

**Target audience**
- Datacenter developers, operators, hyperscale-adjacent teams, AI compute projects

## 3) Market Intelligence
Konative tracks route, market, power, datacenter, and supplier signals to help buyers understand where connectivity demand is forming and where network constraints may affect timing or cost.

**What's included**
- Market briefs and route research
- Datacenter and power signal monitoring
- Coverage and supplier mapping
- Public intelligence feed and weekly briefing surfaces

**Target audience**
- Infrastructure buyers, investors, developers, operators, advisors

## 4) Project Management
Konative stays involved after supplier selection to help orders move through provisioning, installation, escalation, and renewal cycles.

**What's included**
- Installation coordination
- Carrier escalation support
- Renewal and lifecycle review
- Ongoing supplier performance context

**Target audience**
- Buyers who need one point of accountability across multiple suppliers
