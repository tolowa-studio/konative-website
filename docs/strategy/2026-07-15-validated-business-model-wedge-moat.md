## Business model (validated)

### Real shape (stripped of branding)

Konative is a **commission-only connectivity origination desk**: find a triggered buyer, force a competitive multi-supplier process, register in AVANT Pathfinder, ride install → residual. No inventory, no CAPEX, no managed-service P&L. Suppliers pay; buyers do not.

### Unit economics

| Element | Value | Confidence | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Carrier residual to channel | typically 15–20% of MRC | Confirmed (industry) | [Rev.io agent commissions](https://www.rev.io/blog/telecom-agent-commissions) |
| Effective take after TSD | ~8–14% of customer MRC (50–70% pass-through assumption) | **Unconfirmed for Konative’s AVANT contract** | Prior STORM; must verify evergreen + split in agreement |
| Example residual | 100G wave ~$2k MRC → ~$160–$400/mo at effective take | Inferred | Lightyear public price bands + residual math |
| Cash bridge | UCaaS / SPIFF one-time (often 1–6× MRC) | Confirmed (category) | Channel SPIFF practice |
| Book asset | Residuals portable if evergreen + assignable | Confirmed (category) | Agency valuation practice; UPSTACK books |

**Survival rule (inferred, high stakes):** year-1 cash requires either (a) SPIFF-heavy closes or (b) high-MRC installs — not a stack of $700 DIA deals from cold email. Solo break-even still ≈ $80–100k MRC under management over 24–30 months (prior STORM).

**Gate before scaling origination:** open the AVANT sub-agent agreement and confirm **evergreen residuals + assignability**. If either is weak, the “book is the asset” thesis fails.
